Smart Energy Saving Tips

Reduce electricity consumption with simple daily habits and energy-efficient appliances.

Explore Tips

Why Save Electricity?

Saving electricity reduces your monthly bill, conserves natural resources, and helps protect the environment.

Use LED Bulbs

LED bulbs consume up to 80% less electricity and last much longer than traditional bulbs.

Unplug Devices

Disconnect chargers and appliances when they are not in use to avoid standby power consumption.

Maintain AC

Clean air filters regularly and keep AC temperature between 24°C and 26°C.

Use Daylight

Open curtains during the day and use natural sunlight instead of electric lighting.

Switch Off Electronics

Turn off TVs, computers and other electronics completely instead of leaving them in standby mode.

5-Star Appliances

Buy BEE 5-Star rated appliances as they consume much less electricity.

Full Load Washing

Run your washing machine only with a full load to reduce electricity and water usage.

Regular Maintenance

Keep appliances clean and serviced regularly for better performance and lower energy consumption.

✅ Do's

  • Use LED lighting.
  • Switch off unused appliances.
  • Use natural daylight whenever possible.
  • Keep AC temperature at 24–26°C.
  • Purchase energy-efficient appliances.

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't leave chargers plugged in.
  • Don't keep lights ON unnecessarily.
  • Don't overload extension boards.
  • Don't ignore appliance maintenance.
  • Don't keep refrigerator door open for long.

Frequently Asked Questions
