About EcoVolt

Powering Smarter Energy Decisions

EcoVolt is a smart electricity bill estimator designed to help households understand electricity consumption, estimate monthly bills and encourage energy-efficient living.

Estimate Your Bill

Our Story

Many families struggle to estimate their monthly electricity expenses because they don't know how much power each appliance consumes.

EcoVolt was created to solve this problem through a simple, accurate and user-friendly electricity bill estimation system. Instead of complex calculations, users can simply select their appliances, enter usage hours and instantly receive an estimated monthly electricity bill.

Our Mission

To empower every household with an easy-to-use electricity estimation platform that promotes energy awareness, cost savings and sustainable living.

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital solution that encourages smarter electricity consumption and contributes towards a greener future.
