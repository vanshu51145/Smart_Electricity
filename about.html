Our Story

Managing household electricity expenses can be challenging because most users don't know how much electricity each appliance consumes.

EcoVolt was created to simplify this process by providing an easy-to-use electricity bill estimation platform. Users can add appliances, calculate monthly electricity usage and receive an estimated bill within seconds.

Our aim is to make electricity consumption easier to understand while encouraging responsible and energy-efficient living.

Our Mission

To provide a simple, fast and accurate electricity bill estimation platform that helps households reduce electricity costs and adopt smarter energy-saving habits.

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital solution that promotes sustainable energy usage through technology, awareness and intelligent electricity management.
